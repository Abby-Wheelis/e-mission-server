CanBikeCo App Installation and Set up

Instructions

Select the video based on your phone type and follow the video guidelines.

1. Read and accept the privacy policy by clicking on the "I accept" button.

2. Allow access to "Always" Location, Motion & Fitness activity, and Notifications.

3. Use the token given by your program administrator when "Login via token" window is prompted.

4. Select a username.

5. Complete the onboarding survey.
