Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the Can Do Colorado eBike Program?

This program is a partnership between the Colorado Energy Office, Bicycle Colorado, and Northeast Transportation Connections and is designed to demonstrate that electric bikes (eBikes) are a safe, healthy, and convenient way to take essential trips around town. Program participants receive an eBike and accessories at no cost.
 
As part of the Can Do Colorado eBike Program, participants will use the CanBikeCo App on their smartphone to provide insight into travel habits and CO2 savings generated by switching from automobile and transit trips to trips on an eBike. The feedback and data collected via the CanBikeCo App will be crucial to making the future of this program successful.

Does the app need to be on the entire time?

Yes, although you don't need to be using it the entire time, the app needs to be open in the background in order for it to track your data. This means that you should NOT force-kill the app.
 
Participants will also be asked to complete surveys using the app on a regular basis.

Where will my data be used?

This research study is monitored by the Colorado Energy Office, Bicycle Colorado, NETC and the National Renewable Energy Lab (NREL). Your information will not be shared outside of those partners, and it will not be used to track you in any way. It is only to be used for the purpose of this study and will not be distributed in any way.

Your data is being put to good use, helping us better the way Coloradans travel around our state.

How long am I expected to use the CanBikeCo App?

Participants are expected to use the app through the end of 2020, but are welcome to continue providing feedback beyond that as well.
